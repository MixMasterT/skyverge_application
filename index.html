    What was your last online purchase? What steps did you take in that process?

    The last thing I bought online was three roundtrip airplane tickets

    1. I checked pricing through several carriers and aggregator websites to get a general sense of what was available.
    2. I noted which carriers were offering direct-flight service between my destinations and the approximate prices for tickets with each one.
    3. I checked the baggage cost policies for the airlines offering direct-flight service along my desired route.
    4. I recognized that two free bags would be worth over one-hundred dollars per round-trip since we will have lots of luggage, and that this benefit made one carrier (Southwest) much more cost-effective than the others.
    5. I then checked the listing for sale-fares on Southwest, and noticed that one of the airports in my vicinity had a discounted direct-flight fare to my destingation.
    6. I then reserved the tickets.
    7. Before finalizing the purchase, I confirmed the itinerary with my travel partners.
